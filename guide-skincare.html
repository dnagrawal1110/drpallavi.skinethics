SKIN CONCERNS & SOLUTIONS
🎭
Concern 01

Pigmentation & Uneven Skin Tone

Best Ingredients
Alpha Arbutin Tannic Acid

These ingredients help reduce excess melanin production and gradually improve uneven skin tone — particularly effective for the hyperpigmentation common in Indian skin.

How to Use
1Use as a serum — morning or night
2Apply to clean, dry skin before moisturiser
3Always use sunscreen during the day — without SPF, pigmentation will return
🕰️
Concern 02

Wrinkles & Fine Lines

Best Ingredients
Retinol Copper Peptides

These support collagen production and improve skin texture over time. Retinol accelerates cell turnover; copper peptides stimulate collagen and elastin from within the dermis.

How to Use
1Retinol: night use only — start 2–3 times a week
2Begin with a low concentration (0.025–0.05%) if you're new to retinol
3Always follow with SPF the next morning — retinol increases sun sensitivity
⚠️ Do not use retinol if pregnant. Consult a dermatologist before starting.
✨
Concern 03

Dark Underarms

Best Ingredient
Glycolic Acid 6–12%

Gently exfoliates dead skin cells and helps improve discoloration in the underarm area. 6% is recommended for beginners; up to 12% for those with experience using AHAs.

How to Use
1Night use only
22–3 times a week — not daily
3Always patch test before first use
⚠️ Avoid use immediately after shaving or waxing — wait 24 hours.
🔵
Concern 04

Skin Spots & Acne Marks

Best Ingredients
Salicylic Acid 12% Lactic Acid

Salicylic acid cleans pores and reduces active breakouts. Lactic acid improves surface texture and brightens post-acne marks. Together, they address both cause and consequence.

Best For
✓Oily and acne-prone skin
✓Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (dark spots after pimples)
✓Textured, congested pores
⚠️ Do not use both acids on the same night — alternate them for better results and less irritation.
👁️
Concern 05

Dark Circles

Best Ingredients
Arbutin Low-strength Retinol

Arbutin targets pigmentation under the eyes. A very low-strength retinol (0.01–0.025%) can improve skin texture and fine lines around the eye area when used carefully.

How to Use
1Use a rice-grain amount — the under-eye area requires very little product
2Gently tap (do not rub) around the under-eye area using your ring finger
3Night use only for retinol-containing products
⚠️ The under-eye skin is the thinnest on the face. Avoid overuse and always patch test.

SIMPLE DOS & DON'TS
✔ Do
✔Patch test every new product before full use
✔Use sunscreen every single morning
✔Introduce one new active at a time
✔Give products 4–6 weeks before judging results
✕ Don't
✕Mix multiple strong acids on the same night
✕Use retinol without sunscreen the next morning
✕Expect overnight results from any active
✕Use prescription-strength actives without guidance
