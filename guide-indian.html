MISTAKES WE MAKE WITH INDIAN HAIR
✕
Over-oiling overnight (24+ hours): Clogs pores, attracts dust, worsens dandruff. Oil should be washed off within 30–60 minutes.
✕
Ignoring hard water: High-mineral water creates a "crunchy" mineral crust on hair. Most Indian cities have hard water — yet most routines ignore it.
✕
Rough towel drying: Rubbing with a heavy cotton towel causes friction → frizz and split ends. Pat dry gently instead.
✕
Skipping conditioner: Many believe conditioner causes hair fall. It's the opposite — it seals the cuticle, reducing breakage.
✕
Copying Western hair routines: Built for low humidity, soft water, and different hair textures. Doesn't account for our heat, pollution, or water quality.
THE IDEAL INDIAN HAIR ROUTINE
📆

Weekly Wash Day (1–2 times)

1
Pre-wash oil: Apply warm coconut or almond oil to scalp and lengths. Leave for 30–60 minutes only — not overnight.
2
Shampoo the scalp: Focus on scalp only. Massage gently with finger pads, not nails.
3
Condition the lengths: From mid-length to ends. Leave 2–3 minutes. Never apply to scalp.
4
Cold rinse: Final rinse with cooler water seals the cuticle — adds shine and reduces frizz.
5
Pat dry gently: Use a microfibre towel or old T-shirt. Never rub aggressively.
🧪

Monthly (Hard Water Cities)

1
Chelating shampoo wash: Once a month, replace your regular shampoo with a chelating (clarifying) shampoo to remove mineral buildup from hard water.

Daily Habits 🌅

🪮
Detangle with a wide-tooth comb, starting from ends upwards. Never on bone-dry hair.
☀️
If stepping out in sun, apply a light serum or wear a scarf to protect lengths from UV damage.
🛏️
Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase — reduces friction and cuticle damage overnight.
FEED YOUR HAIR FROM INSIDE

Your hair is the last to receive nutrients. 🌿

If you're deficient anywhere, hair is the first to suffer. No serum replaces daily nutrition.

🥚
Protein (Every meal)
Paneer, dal, eggs, soya, sprouts — hair is 90% protein (Keratin).
🥬
Iron & Ferritin
Spinach, dates, jaggery, beetroot. Iron deficiency is the #1 hair fall cause in India.
🌰
Omega-3 & Zinc
Walnuts, flaxseeds, pumpkin seeds — maintains hair shine and reduces shedding.
STYLING HABITS TO AVOID
🎀
Tight hairstyles: Constant tight buns or braids lead to "Traction Alopecia" — thinning at the hairline that can become permanent.
🌡️
Heat without protection: Never use a straightener or dryer without a heat-protectant spray. High heat degrades keratin permanently.
⚗️
Chemical overload: Frequent rebonding or smoothing breaks the natural protein bonds of Indian hair — causing long-term dryness and fragility.
🚿
Very hot water washes: Strips natural oils and opens the cuticle further, allowing more mineral absorption from hard water.
🌟 DO'S & DON'TS
✅

DO

Use clarifying shampoo once a month if in a hard water city
Oil hair — but wash off within an hour
Eat a protein-rich breakfast every day
Dilute shampoo slightly before applying to scalp
End every wash with a cold rinse
🚫

DON'T

Comb wet hair — it's at its weakest then
Use very hot water — strips natural oils
Ignore a dry, itchy scalp — it's inflammation
Apply conditioner to the scalp — lengths only
Leave oil on for more than 1–2 hours
SEE A DERMATOLOGIST IF
⚠️ Consult Now

Don't delay if you notice:

  • Visible thinning or partition widening over months
  • Persistent, itchy dandruff or crusty patches on scalp
  • Hair fall accompanied by weight changes or irregular periods
  • Circular bald patches (Alopecia Areata)
