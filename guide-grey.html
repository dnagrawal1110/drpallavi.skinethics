WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENS WHEN HAIR GOES GREY

Grey baal sirf rang nahi badalta — structure badal jaata hai 🔬

Grey baalon ke baare mein ek aisi cheez batani hai jo bahut log nahi jaante — aapka grey hair ek Hollow Glass Tube jaisa ho jaata hai. Jab baalon mein se pigment (Melanin) chala jaata hai, wo sirf colour nahi badalta. Melanin sirf ek dye nahi tha — woh hair cortex ka structural filler tha.

Melanin hair ke andar ke space ko fill karta tha, structural density provide karta tha. Jab woh jaata hai, hair shaft andar se literally hollow ho jaata hai. Ye hollow pipe jaisi structure ki wajah se grey hair fundamentally weak aur highly reactive ban jaata hai — heat, chemicals, aur environmental damage bahut zyada absorb hote hain.

Think of it as a solid steel rod vs a hollow copper pipe — same thickness, same outer appearance, lekin hollow one bends aur breaks far more easily. Yahi aapke grey baalon ke saath ho raha hai — aur isliye woh differently behave karte hain, different care maangten hain.

📊 The Structural Difference

Dono strands equal thickness ke hain baahir se — lekin andar completely alag hai.

⚫
Pigmented Hair
Solid cortex — melanin fills every gap. Dense, flexible, heat-resistant.
⚪
Grey Hair
Hollow cortex — melanin gone. Air-filled, brittle, highly reactive to heat.
THE HEAT PROBLEM — WHY BLOW-DRY FRIES GREY HAIR
"A hollow glass tube heats from the inside when blow-dried. Your grey hair is doing exactly this — and it's why it turns broom-like."

Hollow pipe mein heat baahir se nahi, andar se build hoti hai. Blow dryer ki heat grey hair ke hollow shaft mein enter hoti hai aur andar ek enclosed space mein concentrate ho jaati hai — bilkul oven ki tarah. Yeh temperature spikes cause karte hain jo protein bonds tod dete hain.

Grey hair ke liye safe temperature pigmented hair ke liye safe temperature se significantly lower hai. Isliye same blow dryer settings jo purane baalon ko fine karte thhe, ab grey baalon ko glass ki tarah brittle aur broom-like bana dete hain. Lower heat hai asal solution — koi bhi product is physics ko bypass nahi kar sakta.

SIGNS YOU'RE MISHANDLING GREY HAIR

Agar yeh signs familiar lagte hain, grey hair ki specific needs address nahi ho rahi hain.

●Breakage same length par baar baar hota hai — hair ek certain length se nahi badhta
●Broom-like appearance ya stiffness — baal wiry aur rough feel karte hain
●Static electricity — grey hair cling karta hai kapdon aur face par
●Colour treatments unexpected damage karti hain — over-penetration ki wajah se
●Conditioning ke baad bhi shine nahi aata — surface treatment andar nahi pohonchta
●Baal dry aur dull feel karte hain even after oiling aur masking
WHAT DOESN'T WORK FOR GREY HAIR
✕

Regular shampoo and conditioner only

Surface moisturisation ek hollow shaft fill nahi karti. Conditioner sirf outer cuticle coat karta hai — andar kuch nahi pohonchta. Grey hair ko structural fillers chahiye, sirf surface hydration nahi.

✕

Same heat settings as before going grey

Grey hair 30–40% lower temperature par fry hona shuru ho jaata hai. High heat = hollow shaft ke andar protein literally cook hona shuru ho jaata hai. Broom-like texture iska result hai.

✕

Same colour treatments as pigmented hair

Grey hair hollow shaft mein chemicals over-penetrate karte hain. Developer concentrations jo pigmented hair ko fine karte hain, grey hair ko over-process aur damage karte hain. Formulation change karna padta hai.

THE INSIDE-OUT APPROACH — WHAT ACTUALLY HELPS
1

🔗 Bond-Building Treatments

Ingredients like bis-aminopropyl diglycol dimaleate (found in Olaplex aur similar bond builders) broken disulfide bonds ko connect karte hain — andar se. Grey hair mein pigmented hair se zyada broken bonds hote hain. Bond builders is structural gap ko fill karte hain properly.

Monthly treatment recommended
2

🧬 Protein Treatments — Hollow Shaft Fill Karo

Keratin, hydrolyzed wheat protein, ya amino acids grey hair ke hollow cortex mein structural material provide karte hain. Yeh physically shaft ko fill karte hain — iska matlab zyada density, less brittleness, aur broom-like texture ka dramatic reduction. Weekly use karni chahiye protein treatment grey hair mein.

Weekly
3

🌡️ Heat Reduction — Lower Settings, Always

Grey hair ke liye blow dryer aur straightener temperature maximum 150°C (300°F) tak limit karo — pigmented hair ke liye normal 180–220°C se kaafi kam. Cool shot button use karo finish ke liye. Heat protectant mandatory hai — koi exception nahi.

Every heat styling session
4

☀️ UV Protection Leave-in — Daily

Grey hair mein melanin nahi hai — jo UV radiation absorb aur neutralise karta tha. Ab UV direct shaft tak pohonchti hai aur grey hair ko further oxidise aur yellow/dull karta hai. UV filter wala leave-in daily use karna essential hai, indoor mein bhi.

Daily — even indoors
5

💜 Toning Shampoo — Yellowing Combat Karo

Grey aur white hair time ke saath yellow ya brassy ho jaate hain — environmental pollutants, minerals, aur oxidation ki wajah se. Purple ya blue toning shampoo (violet pigments) yellowing neutralise karte hain optically. Week mein ek baar regular shampoo ki jagah use karo — zyada nahi, warna baal purple tinge aa jaata hai.

Once a week in place of regular shampoo
