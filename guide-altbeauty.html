THE PROBLEM I WAS SEEING EVERY DAY

Even as a dermatologist, I was prescribing 7–10 product routines to my patients. And then I watched what happened.

They were confused. Spending too much. The routine was so complicated it simply wasn't being followed. No single formulation was complete on its own. And skin wasn't improving.

I stopped. And asked myself a simple question: what if just 2 products could do everything?

"Skin doesn't need treatment. It needs maintenance. One product in the morning. One product at night. That's a complete routine."
— Dr. Pallavi Ahire-Shelke, Founder, altBeauty
WHY COMPLICATED ROUTINES FAIL
❌ Too many steps = lower compliance. You skip it when life gets busy.
❌ Multiple actives can conflict. More isn't always better — sometimes it's harmful.
❌ Spending more doesn't mean better results. It usually means more confusion.
WHAT 2 PRODUCTS CAN DO
☀️ Morning Product
Protect + Hydrate. Shields against UV, pollution, and daily damage while keeping skin moisturised throughout the day.
🌙 Night Product
Repair + Restore. Works with your skin's natural overnight renewal process — targeting concerns while you sleep.
