THE 4 ESSENTIAL INGREDIENTS 🧪
🌿
Ingredient 01

Niacinamide (5%) Serum

What It Does
Controls oil Fights breakouts Brightens tone Minimal irritation

The multitasking hero for teenage skin. Safe enough for daily use and effective enough to actually make a difference — niacinamide tackles breakouts, redness, and dullness all at once.

How to Use It
1Apply a few drops after washing your face
2Lightly pat onto clean skin — do not rub
3Use morning and night
4Apply before moisturiser
5Always finish with sunscreen during the day
Pro Tip
Start with a low concentration (2–5%). Patch test first. Slow and steady wins clear skin.
💧
Ingredient 02

Hyaluronic Acid Serum

What It Does
Deep hydration No oiliness Softer texture All skin types

Hydration without the grease. Hyaluronic acid keeps skin soft, plump, and balanced without triggering breakouts — perfect for all skin types including oily and acne-prone.

How to Use It
1Pat a few drops onto damp skin after washing
2Use morning and night
3Follow with a lightweight, oil-free moisturiser
4Always finish with sunscreen during the day
Pro Tip
Always apply on damp skin — it pulls water into the skin cells. On dry skin, the effect is much weaker.
🫧
Ingredient 03

Gel-Based Moisturiser

Why It Works for Teen Skin
Oily skin Acne-prone Combination skin Non-greasy

Light, oil-free, and quick-absorbing — gel moisturisers keep skin soft and shine-free without clogging pores. Even oily skin needs hydration to stay balanced and healthy.

How to Use It
1Apply a thin layer after cleansing and serum
2Use twice daily — morning and night
3Focus on T-zone, cheeks, and forehead
4Suitable under sunscreen and makeup
Pro Tip
Even oily skin needs moisturiser. Skipping it causes your skin to produce more oil to compensate.
☀️
Ingredient 04 · Most Important

Sunscreen SPF 50+

Why It's Non-Negotiable
Prevents sunburn Stops pigmentation Anti-ageing Daily must-have

Sunscreen is the single most important skincare step at any age. It protects your skin from UV damage, prevents dark spots, and keeps your skin youthful for the long run. No excuses.

How to Use It
1Apply 1 finger-tip unit on face
2Apply at least 15 minutes before sun exposure
3Reapply every 4 hours when outdoors
4Reapply immediately after swimming or sweating
5Last step of your morning routine

USE SUNSCREEN DAILY — Your future skin will thank you.

TIPS FOR SUCCESS 💡

✨ Keep It Minimal

Don't start many actives at once. A simple, consistent routine beats a complicated one every time.

🆕 One at a Time

Introduce one new product at a time. Give each 2 weeks before adding another — so you know what's working.

☀️ SPF is Sacred

Always in the morning. Reapply outdoors. No exceptions, no excuses.

⏰ Be Patient

4–6 weeks for visible results. Consistency is everything. Skin doesn't transform overnight.

WHAT TO EXPECT 📊

Week 1–2

Skin adjusts to the routine. You may notice minor purging — this is normal and temporary. Hydration improves noticeably.

Week 3–4

Oil control starts getting better. Breakouts begin reducing. Skin texture starts to improve.

Week 5–6

Visible brightness and glow. More even skin tone. Smoother texture. The kind of results that build confidence. ✨

QUICK ANSWERS
Q Do I really need a routine if I'm young?
Yes! Good habits now = amazing skin later. Prevention is far easier than repair.
Q My skin is oily — should I skip moisturiser?
No. Oily skin needs hydration too. Use a gel-based, non-comedogenic moisturiser — it will actually reduce oiliness over time.
Q Can I skip sunscreen on cloudy days?
Never. UV rays penetrate clouds. Daily sunscreen is essential 365 days a year.
Q How long before I see results?
Give it 4–6 weeks of consistent use. Visible changes take time — but they do come.
Q What if a product causes irritation?
Stop immediately and consult a dermatologist. Never push through skin irritation.
